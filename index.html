About Gary Kempton

English materials writing

Gary has written a lot of materials over the years for both learning and testing and is the author and producer of the audio-book, 'Mastering Phrasal Verbs Like a Native English Speaker Volume 1', a 3 hour audio-book (with pdf) designed for busy students who want to learn and master over 100 phrasal verbs in a stress-free way.

Teaching

He has also been a freelance online ESL teacher since 2011, working for Learnlight LCC,based in Madrid. His students have worked for Oracle EMEA, 3M, and International Paper He's had students from Europe, the Americas, Asia, and Africa.

Previous to his online work, he was an English teacher in China, for over a year.

Testing

Test writer, Taiwan LTTC. During his early days of teaching, he wrote several tests at intermediate and advanced level for students in Taiwan

Web developing

Since 2019, Gary has been learning web development, specializing in Javascript.
